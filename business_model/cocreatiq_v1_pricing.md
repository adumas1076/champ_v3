## Cocreatiq Pricing

### Setup Fee: $3,000 (one-time)

**What the client gets:**
- Brand audit + content strategy session
- 4 platform accounts configured
- Brand asset templates built (Figma → Freepik/ComfyUI → video editor)
- Content pillars + hook library + script templates created
- First week of content generated + queued
- All integrations wired (Nango OAuth, email, CRM)
- Go-live support + first 48hr monitoring
- DDO baseline established

**Why $3K:**
- Filters tire-kickers (serious buyers only)
- Covers real setup work (8-12 hours of configuration)
- Creates commitment (clients who pay upfront churn 60% less)
- Positions as partner, not tool vendor
- At 5 new clients/month = $15K/mo recurring setup revenue

---

### Tier 1: $500/month

**Who it's for:** Solo creators making $5K+/mo who need their marketing to run without them

**What they get:**
- 1 AI face (their clone)
- 3 platforms (Twitter + IG + 1 more)
- 9 posts/day
- Content Engine (generate → eval → schedule → post)
- Eval quality gate (75%+ score)
- Brand asset templates (basic)
- Autoresearch loop (self-improving content)
- Basic DDO (which post → which click)
- Email lead capture + nurture sequence
- Basic lead scoring
- Community access

---

### Tier 2: $1,000/month

**Who it's for:** Businesses making $20K+/mo who need full attribution + intelligence

**What they get:**
Everything in Tier 1 PLUS:
- 3 AI faces
- 4 platforms
- 12 posts/day
- Full DDO attribution chain (post → click → lead → email → revenue)
- Thumbnail + title intelligence
- Outlier score (find bangers in your niche)
- DM automation (keyword triggers + auto-responder)
- BANT + Priestley lead scoring
- Video editor preview + approve mode
- Priority support
- Full brand asset library

---

### Tier 3: $2,000/month

**Who it's for:** Agencies managing multiple brands, businesses making $50K+/mo

**What they get:**
Everything in Tier 2 PLUS:
- Unlimited AI faces
- All platforms + YouTube
- 36+ posts/day
- Cross-client DDO learning (patterns from ALL clients improve YOUR results)
- Custom scoring model
- White-label dashboard
- API access
- Full video editor + custom templates
- Email + DM + SMS automation
- Competitor tracking
- Dedicated support + monthly strategy call

## Cost to Serve Per Client

| Component | Monthly Cost |
|-----------|-------------|
| LLM (tiered Cortex) | ~$5 |
| Voice (Qwen3-TTS Modal) | ~$15 |
| Visual gen (Freepik V1 / ComfyUI V2) | ~$5-25 |
| Video render (Modal compute) | ~$8 |
| Captions (Whisper) | ~$3 |
| Storage + CDN | ~$2 |
| Nango + email (Resend) | ~$2 |
| **Total cost to serve** | **~$40-60/client/month** |

**Margins:**
- Tier 1 ($500): ~$450 profit = 90% margin
- Tier 2 ($1,000): ~$950 profit = 95% margin
- Tier 3 ($2,000): ~$1,950 profit = 97% margin

**SaaS margins at scale. The more clients, the cheaper it gets (shared compute, cross-client DDO learning).**
