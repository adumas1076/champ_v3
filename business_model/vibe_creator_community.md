## Growth Model: 100 → 1,000 Members

### Phase 1: Foundation (Members 1-50) — $5K/mo

**Focus:** Prove the value. Build the core group.

| What Anthony Does | Effort |
|-------------------|--------|
| 1 live vibe session/week (1 hr) | 4 hrs/mo |
| Post 2-3 times/week in community | 2 hrs/mo |
| Reply to member posts when he feels like it | 1 hr/mo |
| **Total time commitment** | **~7 hrs/mo** |

**What members get:**
- Access to Anthony's creative process in real-time
- Weekly live session (vibe coding, vibe designing, vibe building — whatever Anthony's working on)
- Community feed (share work, get feedback, connect)
- The vibe creator skill list as a framework

**Revenue:** 50 × $100 = $5,000/mo
**Effort:** ~7 hrs/mo = $714/hr effective rate

**How to get 50 members:**
- Creator Create content drives awareness (free)
- CTA on every post: "Join the Vibe Creators — link in bio"
- First 50 = your warmest audience (existing followers, email list, DMs)
- No paid ads needed at this stage

### Phase 2: Momentum (Members 50-150) — $15K/mo

**Focus:** Social proof kicks in. Members bring members.

| What Changes | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Add: member spotlights | Feature what members CREATE (proof of transformation) |
| Add: vibe challenges | Weekly creation challenge (best one gets featured) |
| Add: resource library | Save replays + Anthony's templates/assets |
| Same: 1 live/week | Don't increase Anthony's time — protect the pace |

**Revenue:** 150 × $100 = $15,000/mo
**Effort:** ~10 hrs/mo = $1,500/hr effective rate

**How to grow 50 → 150:**
- Member referrals (happy members tell friends)
- Creator Create content keeps feeding the top of funnel
- Member wins become content (screenshot their results → post → new members see proof)
- "I'm a Vibe Creator" becomes an identity people want

### Phase 3: Engine (Members 150-300) — $30K/mo

**Focus:** Community runs itself. Anthony shows up because he wants to, not because he has to.

| What Changes | Details |
|-------------|---------|

**Revenue:** 300 × $100 = $30,000/mo
**Effort:** ~8 hrs/mo (community self-sustains) = $3,750/hr effective rate

**How to grow 150 → 300:**
- Community momentum (active community attracts more)
- Cocreatiq clients join community for the mindset layer
- Podcast/YouTube features members → their audience joins
- "I Am A Vibe Creator" merch/identity drives belonging
