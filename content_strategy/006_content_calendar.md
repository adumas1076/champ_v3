## WEEKLY THEMES (The Narrative Arc)

Each day has a **primary pillar focus** that creates a weekly rhythm. Audience learns to expect certain content on certain days.

| Day | Primary Pillar | Bucket Focus | Energy | Series |
|-----|---------------|--------------|--------|--------|
| **MONDAY** | Creator → Business Owner | Mindset / Origin | Motivation — start the week with identity | Me & Champ ep |
| **TUESDAY** | Vibe Creating | Contrarian / Mindset | Challenge the norm — redefine the lane | TBTNTT ep |
| **WEDNESDAY** | The Marketing Machine | Education / Proof | Systems day — teach something scalable | Me & Champ ep |
| **THURSDAY** | Building in Public | BTS / Adversity | Real talk — show the build | TBTNTT ep |
| **FRIDAY** | The Creative Process | Education / BTS | Craft day — creative deep-dive | YouTube drops |
| **SATURDAY** | Mixed (Community / Lifestyle) | Community / Lifestyle | Breathing day — celebrate members/vision | Optional TBTNTT |
| **SUNDAY** | External (trends + news) | External / Contrarian | Reaction day — comment on the week's events | — (rest from series) |

**Why this rhythm:**
- Monday = audience comes back to the week ready for mindset content
- Tuesday/Thursday = contrarian hot-take days (algorithms reward sharp takes mid-week)
- Wednesday = educational anchor (people actively looking to learn)
- Friday = craft/creative content (audience relaxing, more visual)
- Saturday = lighter energy, celebration
- Sunday = trend reaction (weekend news/industry drops)

## MONTHLY STRATEGY

### Week 1: Foundation
- Introduce yourself to new followers every 7 days
- Heavy TOFU (attract)
- Series episodes start hitting regular rhythm
- First YouTube pillar drops (Friday)

### Week 2: Depth
- Trust content increases (MOFU deepens)
- First BOFU push mid-week
- YouTube pillar 2 → repurpose machine ramps

### Week 3: Conversion
- BOFU peaks (2 posts this week)
- Testimonials + case studies featured
- Direct offers + DM keywords pushed

### Week 4: Review + Reset
- Analytics review (which content is winning?)
- Lessons posted publicly (BTS insights)
- Plan month 2 based on data
- Autoresearch loop updates rules

### Monthly Rotation
- Each month, ONE pillar becomes the "lead pillar" (gets 30% of content instead of 20%)
- Rotation prevents pillar fatigue
- Lets each pillar get a deep-dive month

| Month | Lead Pillar | Why |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Month 1 | Vibe Creating | Define the lane |
| Month 2 | Building in Public | Show the work |
| Month 3 | The Marketing Machine | Demo the product |
| Month 4 | Creator → Business Owner | Transformation story |
| Month 5 | The Creative Process | Unique edge |
| Month 6 | Rotate back to Vibe Creating (the anchor) | Re-anchor |

## THE CONTENT FACTORY WORKFLOW (Daily Execution)

```
EVERY MORNING (automated):
  1. System checks calendar → what pillar + bucket for today?
  2. Pulls topic from Topic Bank (unused, matches pillar + funnel)
  3. Pulls hook from Hook Library (matches hook type + KLT)
  4. Runs through 12-step Content Formula
  5. Generates 3 platform-native versions
  6. Eval.py quality gate (must pass 75%+)
  7. Schedules for optimal time on each platform
  8. Posts automatically (Auto Mode) or queues for review (Self Mode)

48 HOURS AFTER EACH POST:
  1. Scoring.py pulls analytics
  2. Calculates weighted score
  3. Hook Rate gate (must hit 25%+)
  4. Compares to benchmarks (yours + niche + top 10%)
  5. Feeds back to autoresearch loop
  6. Updates hook/topic/bucket effectiveness scores

WEEKLY (automated):
  - Outlier research (find 5-10x performers in watch list)
  - Topic bank refresh (add new topics based on trends)
  - Monthly peak time adjustment (after Week 4)
  - Series episode drop tracking (consistency check)

MONTHLY:
  - Pillar performance review
  - Lead pillar rotation
  - Quarterly campaign planning
```

## V1 → V2 EVOLUTION

**Week 1-4:** Run generic calendar with research-backed times

**Week 9-12:** Major rebalance — pillars that overperform get more slots, underperformers get cut or refreshed

## Anthony's Tweak Points

When you do the review pass, focus on:
- Daily pillar themes (are Mon/Tue/etc. matched right?)
- Energy rhythm (does the week arc feel right?)
- Monthly lead pillar order (does Month 1 starting with Vibe Creating make sense?)
- Quarterly campaign themes (are they the right Q1-Q4 mix?)

Everything here is adjustable. The STRUCTURE is locked. The CONTENT inside the structure flexes with your taste.
