## UNIVERSAL TITLE PRINCIPLES

### 1. The 8-Word Test
If someone can't get the promise in the first 8 words, they scroll. Front-load the hook.

### 2. The Specificity Rule
- Vague: "How I grew my business"
- Specific: "How I went from $4K/mo to $47K/mo in 6 months"
- Specific wins every single time.

### 3. The Number Trick
Numbers stop the scroll. Especially odd numbers (7, 11, 13) which feel more authentic than round numbers (10, 100). Dollar amounts, timeframes, and quantities all work.

### 4. The Curiosity Gap
The title should create a question that REQUIRES the content to answer. Don't give away the answer in the title.

### 5. The One-Promise Rule
One benefit per title. "Learn to make money and lose weight" = confused. Pick one.

## 15 PROVEN TITLE FORMULAS (The Library)

These are battle-tested patterns from outlier research. The system adapts these to Anthony's voice + topic.

### Formula 1: The Result Formula
**Pattern:** "How I [SPECIFIC RESULT] in [SPECIFIC TIMEFRAME]"
- "How I built an AI OS in 800 sessions"
- "How I went from hired gun to $100K/mo business"
- "How I turned $42 in Modal bills into a working avatar pipeline"

**Best for:** TRUST stage, Proof bucket, YouTube + LinkedIn

### Formula 2: The Contrarian Formula
**Pattern:** "Why [COMMON BELIEF] is wrong" OR "Why [COMMON ADVICE] will ruin you"
- "Why 'just use ChatGPT' advice is destroying new creators"
- "Why scaling by hiring is the biggest trap in 2026"
- "Why learning AI is the wrong goal"

**Best for:** KNOW stage, Contrarian bucket, Twitter + LinkedIn

### Formula 3: The Number + Hook Formula
**Pattern:** "[NUMBER] [THINGS] that [BENEFIT/OUTCOME]"
- "7 AI myths destroying new creators"
- "3 reasons your AI output looks generic"
- "15 Vibe Creator skills nobody's teaching"

**Best for:** Any stage, works on all platforms, YouTube loves it

### Formula 4: The Question Formula
**Pattern:** "Why does [RELATABLE FRUSTRATION] keep happening?"
- "Why does every AI course feel outdated in 3 months?"
- "Why do 'AI gurus' never show real proof?"

**Best for:** KNOW stage, builds curiosity gap, Twitter + TikTok

### Formula 5: The Shocking Statement
**Pattern:** "[BOLD CLAIM that breaks expectations]"
- "Marketing agencies will be obsolete in 24 months"
- "You don't need code to build an app. But that's not the truth."
- "95% of 'AI gurus' are actually consuming AI, not creating with it"

**Best for:** KNOW stage, Contrarian/Shock bucket, Twitter + TikTok

### Formula 6: The Story Hook
**Pattern:** "[NUMBER/TIME] ago I was [BAD SITUATION]. Here's what changed."
- "6 months ago I was trading hours for dollars. Then everything changed."
- "800 sessions ago, my 'AI partner' couldn't even remember my name."

**Best for:** LIKE stage, Origin/Adversity bucket, all platforms

### Formula 7: The Insider Secret
**Pattern:** "What [GROUP] don't tell you about [TOPIC]"
- "What AI influencers don't tell you about building AI products"
- "What '$10K/mo coaches' don't tell you about their actual revenue"

**Best for:** TRUST stage, Contrarian + Education, LinkedIn + Twitter

### Formula 8: The Before/After
**Pattern:** "I used to [BAD STATE]. Now I [GOOD STATE]. Here's how."
- "I used to panic when clients ghosted. Now I panic when I'm IN the business instead of ON it."
- "I used to collect AI apps. Now I build AI systems. Different mindset."

**Best for:** LIKE/TRUST stage, Origin + Mindset, all platforms

### Formula 9: The Framework Reveal
**Pattern:** "The [NAMED] framework for [SPECIFIC OUTCOME]"
- "The Click to Client framework for stranger-to-customer conversion"
- "The Dr. Frankenstein method for building without starting from scratch"
- "The Vibe Creator stack for going from consumer to creator"

**Best for:** TRUST stage, Education bucket, LinkedIn + YouTube

### Formula 10: The Honest Failure
**Pattern:** "I [FAILED THING]. Here's what I learned."
- "I spent $42 on a blurry splat. Best money I've spent."
- "I threw away 2 full versions of Cocreatiq. Here's what V3 fixed."

**Best for:** LIKE stage, Adversity/BTS, builds trust through vulnerability

### Formula 11: The Timeline Drop
**Pattern:** "Day [N] of [ONGOING BUILD]"
- "Day 847 of building an AI operating system"
- "Week 4 of running the Marketing Machine solo"

**Best for:** LIKE stage, BTS/Saga, Twitter + IG + Me & Champ series

### Formula 12: The Contrarian "True But"
**Pattern:** "[COMMON CLAIM]. That's true. But that's not the truth."
- "You don't need to know code to build an app. That's true. But that's not the truth."
- "AI will do the work for you. That's true. But that's not the truth."

**Best for:** KNOW stage, Contrarian bucket, "TBTNTT" series signature

### Formula 13: The Challenge Hook
**Pattern:** "I bet you [CAN'T DO X] without [CONSTRAINT]"
- "I bet you can't build a landing page in 47 minutes without templates"
- "I bet you can't find a marketing tool that does what operators do"

**Best for:** KNOW stage, Challenge bucket, Twitter + TikTok

### Formula 14: The List + Warning
**Pattern:** "[NUMBER] [THINGS] that will [NEGATIVE OUTCOME]"
- "7 marketing moves that will obsolete your agency in 12 months"
- "3 creator habits that will cap your income forever"

**Best for:** KNOW stage, Contrarian, Twitter + LinkedIn

### Formula 15: The Universal Insight
**Pattern:** "Everyone [DOES X]. But [SMARTER ALTERNATIVE] is what actually works."
- "Everyone optimizes prompts. Principles are what actually scale."
- "Everyone posts content. Systematizing content is what actually grows."

**Best for:** KNOW/TRUST stage, Contrarian + Education, LinkedIn + Twitter

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TITLE RULES

### YouTube
- **Length:** 60 characters MAX (longer gets truncated)
- **Front-load:** Most important words in first 30 chars
- **A/B test:** Always — YouTube supports native A/B for titles
- **Numbers:** Almost always win (odd numbers > even)
- **Emotional words:** "stop," "never," "always," "shocking," "proven"
- **Avoid:** Clickbait that doesn't deliver (YouTube punishes low retention)

### Twitter/X
- **Length:** First 8 words matter most
- **Hook in line 1, payoff in line 2+**
- **No hashtags in the hook** — they dilute
- **Numbers work:** especially dollar amounts + timeframes
- **Emoji:** 0-1 max, only if it adds meaning

### Instagram (Caption)
- **First 125 characters:** must hook (cut-off before "see more")
- **Line breaks:** help readability
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 niche tags, NOT the hook
- **Hook pattern:** Question or bold statement

### TikTok (Caption)
- **Shorter is better:** 50-80 characters
- **Match the video's hook:** caption = text version of verbal hook
- **Hashtags:** 3-4 (mix of niche + trending)
- **Front-load curiosity:** "wait for the..." or "if you're [X]..."

### LinkedIn (Post Headline)
- **First 125 characters:** critical (see-more cutoff)
- **Line 1 = hook, line 2 = context, line 3 = "read more"**
- **Professional tone:** but not boring
- **Numbers + specificity:** business owners love data

### Facebook
- **Similar to LinkedIn** but slightly more casual
- **Longer captions work** (older demo reads more)

## A/B TESTING WORKFLOW

### For YouTube Pillar Videos
```
Upload video → generate 3 title variations using different formulas:
  Variation A: Result formula
  Variation B: Contrarian formula
  Variation C: Number + hook formula
  
Let YouTube A/B test natively for 14 days
Keep the winner, use that pattern for similar future videos
```

### For Other Platforms (Pre-score Before Posting)
```
TITLE SCORE =
  First 8 words clarity       (1-10)
  Specificity (numbers/detail) (1-10)
  Curiosity gap strength       (1-10)
  Platform length fit          (1-10)
  Formula match to top outliers (1-10)
  / 5 = average score

Must be 7+ to ship. 9+ is elite.
```
