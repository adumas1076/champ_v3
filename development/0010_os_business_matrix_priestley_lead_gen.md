## Core Framework: The Online Assessment Lead Gen System

**The Thesis** [0:00]
> "Everything is downstream from lead generation. If you can generate leads, you can solve all the other problems after that. But if you cannot generate leads, you are dead in the water."

This system has worked across: agencies, software companies, education/training, coaching, consulting. Every new business project starts with setting up an online assessment.

## The 3-Part System

### Part 1: The Landing Page [0:48]

**Structure (top to bottom):**

**1. The Hook** [1:00]
Two types:

| Hook Type | Formula | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Frustration Hook** | "Feeling frustrated that [problem] even though you [effort]?" | "Feeling frustrated that you're not getting fitness results even though you work out every week?" |
| **Results Hook** | "Are you ready to [desired result]?" | "Are you ready to run your first marathon?" |

- Frustration hook → they want to fix the pain
- Results hook → they want to test their readiness ("Am I ready? Let me take this to find out.")
- *Source: [1:00-2:00]*

**2. Subheading** [2:04]
> "Answer 15 questions to find out why you're experiencing [frustration] and what to do about it."

Directs them to take the assessment.

**3. Value Proposition** [2:20]
> "Take this assessment so we can measure and improve three key areas: [area 1], [area 2], [area 3]."

- Always three areas (three-pillar pattern — same as Hormozi's three-pillar pitch)
- Must be three things people would love to achieve
- *Source: [2:20-2:55]*

**4. Credibility Section** [3:00]
Three elements:
- **Who created this** — your bio, your credentials
- **What you've done** — past results, track record
- **Research** — statistics, studies, data ("85% of people struggle with X")
- *Source: [3:00-3:44]*

**5. Call to Action (CTA)** [3:46]
Must include four elements:
1. "Start the quiz" (the action)
2. "It only takes 3 minutes" (time commitment)
3. "It's completely free" (removes cost barrier)
4. "You get immediate recommendations" (instant value)

**Result:** 20-40% of landing page visitors start the quiz. [4:07]
- *Source: [3:46-4:17]*

### Part 2: The 15 Quiz Questions [4:20]

**Section A: Contact Capture (before questions start)** [4:29]
- Name (required)
- Email (required)
- Location (auto-detected via IP)
- Phone number (optional — don't make it mandatory)

**Section B: 10 Best Practices Questions** [5:00]
- Ask if they're doing 10 things they SHOULD be doing
- Yes/No or scale answers
- This generates their SCORE
- Examples (sleep): "Do you go to bed at the same time?" "Do you avoid screens before bed?"
- Examples (fitness): "Do you track calories?" "Do you work out 3-4x per week?"
- *Source: [5:00-5:44]*

**Section C: The Big 5 Qualifying Questions** [5:46]

| # | Question | What It Tells You | Source |
|---|----------|------------------|--------|
| 1 | "Which best describes your current situation?" | Who they are (student, manager, executive) — segments them | [5:58] |
| 2 | "What is your desired outcome in the next 90 days?" | Their #1 priority — what's driving their decision | [6:23] |
| 3 | "What obstacle is stopping you? / What have you tried that hasn't worked?" | Their pain + past failures (maps to Hormozi's pain cycle) | [6:52] |
| 4 | "Which solution do you think would suit you best?" | Implies their budget. Book = $10. Done-for-you = $10-20K | [7:06] |
| 5 | "Is there anything else we need to know?" (open text box) | Wild card gold. People reveal buying signals, urgency, budget constraints | [7:40] |

**Key Insight on Q5** [7:49]:
> "People will tell you what's driving their decision. I've had people put 'I've already read your book and want to get started' or 'I have a budget but I have to spend it by the end of the month.'"

### Part 3: Dynamic Results Page [8:21]

**The page changes based on how they answered.** Not static — dynamic.

**Structure:**

**1. The Big Reveal** [9:01]
- Overall score/result
- Visual: speedometer, thermometer, percentage
- Dynamic message: "Congratulations, you scored really well" OR "You've got a lot of room to improve" OR "Strong foundations, plenty of room to improve"

**2. Three Insights** [9:07]
- Three personalized takeaways based on their answers
- "As a result of how you answered, here are three things I want you to know"

**3. Next Steps (Dynamic Routing)** [9:15]

| Lead Quality | Next Step Offered |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Highly qualified** | One-to-one meeting / call |
| **Somewhat qualified** | Group presentation / event |
| **Not a fit** | Content — video, podcast episode, book recommendation |

This is automated lead routing based on quiz answers. The system qualifies AND routes.

**4. Contact + Social Links** [9:47]
- Website, social media, all contact info
- They can explore further even if they don't book

## Operator SOP — Lead Gen Operator (Assessment System)

### Setup Phase
1. Identify the frustration hook OR results hook for the business
2. Define 3 key areas the assessment measures (value proposition)
3. Write credibility section (bio, past results, research/stats)
4. Create CTA with all 4 elements (action, time, free, instant value)

### Quiz Design
5. Write 10 best practices questions (yes/no or scale)
6. Add the Big 5 qualifying questions:
   - Current situation
   - Desired outcome (90 days)
   - Obstacle / what hasn't worked
   - Preferred solution type (implies budget)
   - Open text: "Anything else?"
7. Build dynamic results logic:
   - Score thresholds for high/medium/low
   - Three insights per score bracket
   - Next step routing per qualification level

### Operations
8. Monitor: landing page conversion rate (target: 20-40% start quiz)
9. Monitor: quiz completion rate
10. Route leads automatically: hot → call, warm → group event, cold → content
11. Sales team receives full quiz data before first contact
12. Speed to lead: contact within 60 seconds of quiz completion (Hormozi rule)
