## The 7 Brand-Killing Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Confusing Attention for Authority [1:11-3:33]

**The mistake:** Thinking the person with the most followers wins.

**Reality:**
- People with hundreds of thousands (even millions) of followers often "can't sell a single product or service" [1:45-1:50]
- The content that gets millions of followers fast is usually content that WON'T make money [2:24-2:32]
- 200K followers from viral/trendy content < 10K followers who actually care to buy [3:36-3:57]

**Big idea:** "Just because people are watching doesn't mean they trust you" [2:16-2:18]

**ISM:** "Not all attention is good attention" — same as "not all money is good money" [3:25-3:33]

**The back-breaker:** This is the #1 mistake. "If you don't listen to the other six... your one takeaway should be that not all attention is good attention." [3:20-3:27]

- *Source: [1:11-3:33]*

### Mistake 2: Creating Tips Instead of Transformation [4:07-5:37]

**The mistake:** Thinking value = tips, hacks, quick how-tos.

**The shift:** Move from tips to TRANSFORMATION [4:13-4:27]

**What transformation includes:**
- Mental shifts [5:19]
- Belief shifting [5:20]
- Perspective shifting [5:22-5:25]
- Not just how-to's — any shift that gives a new outlook on life [5:13-5:31]

**Why transformation wins:**
- When someone transforms from your content, they become "in debt to you" [4:50-4:52]
- They start looking for your solution on their own — you don't have to sell [4:58-5:02]
- "You get people that buy without even going through your entire sales process — 'I'm going to skip the line'" [5:02-5:08]

**ISM:** "Stop creating tips and start creating transformation" [5:34-5:36]

- *Source: [4:07-5:37]*

### Mistake 3: Skipping Buyer's Awareness (No Content Funnel) [5:38-7:11]

**The mistake:** Posting random, sleazy, salesy content with no purpose — no funnel thinking.

**The fix:** Apply funnel logic to content: [5:48-6:00]
- **Top of funnel content** → Broad audience, awareness
- **Middle of funnel content** → Message shifts, some people drop off (that's fine)
- **Bottom of funnel content** → "You only have a few hundred, a few thousand left, but those thousand will ride or die for you" [6:42-6:44]

**The problem:**
- Most entrepreneurs skip this entirely — they copy what works for others, post whatever they want [6:51-7:03]
- "It's not translating into sales. And it's because you're missing the buyer's awareness." [7:06-7:11]

- *Source: [5:38-7:11]*

---

### Mistake 4: Talking Like a Guru Instead of the Expert You Are [7:14-8:21]

**The mistake:** Entrepreneurs who are established experts in real life "shrink" when they get online [7:55-7:59]

**Reality:**
- "You are the friendly neighborhood of what you do" — people in your city know you, you have clients, you've made money [7:42-7:53]
- "And yet when you get online, you shrink and that message doesn't come across, that confidence doesn't come across" [7:55-8:01]

**The fix:** "That same energy that your clients see, that same energy when you are in team meetings — bring that energy to your content." [8:13-8:21]

- *Source: [7:14-8:21]*

---

### Mistake 5: Telling/Teaching Too Much Without Showing [8:22-9:44]

**The mistake:** Overloading with verbal information — "just yapping without showing" [9:00-9:03]

**Why it fails:**
- "Most of the time they don't care about that" (the talking head yapping) [9:37-9:38]
- "We are visual creatures. And so the more visuals that you can show, the better." [9:40-9:46]

**The fix:** Take the same value and SHOW it: [9:07-9:34]
- Use props
- Use green screen
- Use more photos
- Switch to back camera and show stuff
- Stop relying on front-camera yapping

**Action step:** Find a recent video where you gave great value but just talked — reshoot the SAME value but show it instead [9:08-9:18]

- *Source: [8:22-9:44]*

---

### Mistake 6: Creating From Ego Instead of Empathy [9:49-10:43]

**The mistake:** Creating content that YOU want to make, not what your AUDIENCE wants to see [10:01-10:10]

**The reality check:** [10:13-10:38]
- When you were coming up as an entrepreneur, you searched for specific videos and couldn't find them
- "Why is nobody making a video on this?" [10:26]
- "But you're repeating the cycle" [10:35-10:37]

**ISM:** "Stop being selfish." [10:40-10:43]

- *Source: [9:49-10:43]*

---

### Mistake 7: Copying Trends Instead of Owning Your Own Point of View [10:48-11:08]

**The mistake:** Copying trends without them having true substance for what you stand for and what your audience actually cares about.

**The fix:** "Stop copying so many trends without it having true substance for what you stand for and what your audience actually cares about." [10:55-11:08]

- *Source: [10:48-11:08]*

## The 7 Mistakes as a Diagnostic Matrix

| # | Mistake | Anti-Pattern | Fix |
|---|---------|-------------|-----|
| 1 | Attention ≠ Authority | Chasing follower count | Target the RIGHT followers who buy |
| 2 | Tips ≠ Transformation | Surface-level value | Create mental/belief/perspective shifts |
| 3 | No Buyer's Awareness | Random posting, no funnel | Apply TOF → MOF → BOF to content |
| 4 | Guru Mode | Shrinking online vs. IRL confidence | Bring real-life expert energy to content |

| 7 | Trend Copying | No unique POV | Own your point of view, add substance |

## Operator SOPs Extracted

### Content Operator — Brand Mistake Audit SOP
1. Pull recent 20 posts from entrepreneur's social media
2. Score each post against the 7-mistake matrix:
   - Is it chasing attention or building authority?
   - Does it create transformation or just give tips?
   - Does it fit into a content funnel (TOF/MOF/BOF)?
   - Does it reflect the entrepreneur's real-life expert energy?
   - Does it SHOW or just TELL?
   - Is it serving the audience or the entrepreneur's ego?
   - Is it a trend copy or does it carry the entrepreneur's unique POV?
3. Flag posts that fail 2+ checks
4. Recommend specific reworks using the fix column from the diagnostic matrix
5. Priority order: Fix Mistake 1 first (attention vs authority) — it's the "back-breaker"

### Content Operator — Content Funnel SOP
1. Categorize all content as TOF (awareness), MOF (consideration), or BOF (conversion)
2. Check distribution: healthy ratio is approximately 50% TOF / 30% MOF / 20% BOF
3. If all content is TOF (viral/trends): audience won't buy — add MOF/BOF
4. If all content is BOF (salesy): audience won't grow — add TOF
5. Each funnel stage should still follow the 3 Pillars (one thing, quick wins, aha moments)
