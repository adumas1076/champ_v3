## The Philosophy

> "You don't make money. You create wealth."
> "Timeless principles applied to the most powerful tool ever created."

**Core belief:** Creators CREATE things. AI is the most powerful creation tool ever built. But most people are CONSUMING AI (apps, tutorials, prompts) instead of CREATING with it.

**Principle Sources:**
- **Edward de Bono** — Lateral Thinking (think sideways, not linear. Dr. Frankenstein method.)
- **Donella Meadows** — Systems Thinking (see the whole system, not parts. OS mindset.)
- **Ray Dalio** — Principles (create rules, test them, improve them. Business Matrix.)
- **Anthony (original)** — Universal Success Principles + Creation Philosophy
