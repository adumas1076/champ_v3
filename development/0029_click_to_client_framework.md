## What Is Click to Client?

Click to Client is a universal marketing and sales framework that converts unknown strangers into paying, long-term clients through a systematic pipeline. Every business needs this. Every operator in Cocreatiq OS uses this as the spine.

It's not a feature. It's the **operating system for revenue.**

```
STRANGER → CLICK → LAND → CAPTURE → NURTURE → CONVERT → ONBOARD → ACTIVATE → RETAIN → REFER → LOOP
```

---

## The 3 Funnel Stages (T-M-B OFU)

### TOFU — Top of Funnel (Attract)
**Goal:** Get strangers to notice you and click
**Metric:** Impressions, clicks, CPM, CTR

| Channel | Type | Operator | Hormozi Framework |
|---------|------|----------|-------------------|
| Organic content (4 faces × 5 platforms) | Content Marketing | Marketing Operator | Hook/Retain/Reward, Repurposing Pipeline |
| Paid ads (Meta, YouTube, TikTok) | Paid Ads | Lead Gen Operator | Ad Assembly 70/20/10, 6 angles × 5 hooks |
| Cold outreach (LinkedIn, email) | Cold Outreach | Lead Gen Operator | Rule of 100, Core Four |
| Warm outreach (DMs, network) | Warm Outreach | Sales Operator | Referral asks, past client reactivation |
| SEO / search | Content Marketing | Marketing Operator | Long-form pillar content |

**Smart Funnel Logic:**
- Content auto-scores against eval criteria (Lamar + Gary Vee frameworks)
- Underperformers get rotated, winners get amplified
- Autoresearch loop pulls analytics and self-improves
- Ad creative follows 70/20/10 budget allocation
- 3 AI clones + Anthony = 4 faces, 60-100 pieces/day

---

### MOFU — Middle of Funnel (Engage)
**Goal:** Build trust, demonstrate value, capture contact info
**Metric:** Lead capture rate, email signups, lead magnet downloads

| Component | What It Does | Operator | Hormozi Framework |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| Landing page | First impression — headline, proof, CTA | Marketing Operator | 80% above the fold, incomplete info bias |
| Lead magnet | Free value that solves a narrow problem | Lead Gen Operator | 7-step lead magnet creation, naming formula |
| Social proof | Skipper case study, portfolio, Stripe screenshots | Sales Operator | "If free is this good, paid must be insane" |
| Content drip | Ongoing education via email/content | Marketing Operator | 80% education, 20% CTA |
| Assessment/quiz | "Is Your Business AI-Ready?" | Lead Gen Operator | Priestley assessment system |

**Smart Funnel Logic:**
- Lead magnet auto-generates based on content performance data
- Lead scoring happens at capture (BANT + Priestley)
- Hot leads (score 80+) → immediate Sales Operator routing
- Warm leads (score 50-79) → nurture sequence
- Cold leads (score <50) → content drip, no sales pressure
- Speed-to-lead: < 60 seconds response (operators never sleep)

---

### BOFU — Bottom of Funnel (Convert)
**Goal:** Close the sale, process payment, start the relationship
**Metric:** Conversion rate, average deal value, CAC, time to close

| Component | What It Does | Operator | Hormozi Framework |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| Sales call / chat | Run CLOSER framework | Sales Operator | Clarify → Label → Overview → Sell Vacation → Explain → Reinforce |
| Offer presentation | Grand Slam Offer with enhancers | Sales Operator | Value Equation, 4 enhancers (scarcity/urgency/bonuses/guarantee) |
| Objection handling | AAA method, stack closes | Sales Operator | 4 objection categories, price silence rule |
| Checkout | Stripe payment processing | System | Friction reduction, one-click checkout |
| Post-purchase reinforcement | Prevent buyer's remorse | Onboarding Operator | BAMFAM, personal welcome, identity shift |

**Smart Funnel Logic:**
- Sales Operator adapts pitch based on lead score and source
- Offer enhancers rotate based on conversion data
- Cost estimator shows prospect what their operators will cost BEFORE buying
- Guarantee type selected based on prospect risk profile
- BAMFAM (Book A Meeting From A Meeting) triggers immediately post-close

## Post-Funnel: The Retention Engine

### ONBOARD
| Step | What Happens | Operator | Timeframe |
|------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Welcome | Personal greeting, gift trigger ($100 budget) | Onboarding Operator | Minute 0 |
| Setup | Staff first operator, connect stack (Nango OAuth) | Onboarding Operator | First 30 min |
| Quick Win | First value moment — operator does something useful ON the call | Onboarding Operator | First session |
| Tour | Walk through capabilities, set expectations | Onboarding Operator | Day 1 |
| Check-in | "How's it going?" call/message | Retention Operator | Day 3, 7, 14 |

### ACTIVATE
| Metric | Target | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Time to first value | < 30 minutes | Operator greeting + first task completed on onboarding call |
| Feature adoption (week 1) | 3+ tools used | Guided exploration through operator conversations |
| Return visit (day 7) | 80%+ | Check-in + proactive content delivery |

### RETAIN
| Strategy | Trigger | Operator |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Escalation triage | Frustration detected | Retention Operator |
| Inverse nurture | Usage dropping | Retention Operator (increase contact 1x → 2x → 3x) |
| Proactive downgrade | Usage < 30% for 2 weeks | Retention Operator (offer cheaper plan before they cancel) |
| Ascension offer | High engagement | Sales Operator (upgrade pitch, target 48% ascension rate) |
| Pause option | "I want to cancel" | Retention Operator (offer pause instead) |
| Community bonds | Ongoing | Marketing Operator (shift loyalty from product to community) |

### REFER
| Program | Incentive | Operator |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Standard referral | 20% commission | Automated |
| Behavior-changing | 50% first month + $500 bonus at 10 referrals | Sales Operator |
| Affiliate tier | Tiered rewards, leaderboard, public recognition | Marketing Operator |

## Smart Funnels — What Makes This Different

Traditional funnels are dumb pipes. Click → page → form → email → hope. Static. Same experience for everyone.

Cocreatiq Smart Funnels are **operator-powered, adaptive, and self-improving:**

| Feature | Traditional Funnel | Cocreatiq Smart Funnel |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Lead scoring | Manual or basic rules | AI scoring (BANT + Priestley + behavioral) |
| Response time | Hours to days | < 60 seconds (operators never sleep) |
| Personalization | Segment-based | Individual-based (7-layer memory per prospect) |
| Objection handling | FAQ page | Real-time CLOSER framework via Sales Operator |
| Content | Static drip | Dynamic — autoresearch loop improves content daily |
| Ad optimization | Weekly manual review | 70/20/10 auto-rotation with creative scoring |
| Retention | Churn alert emails | Proactive operator outreach, inverse nurture, pause offers |
| Referrals | "Share this link" | Behavior-changing incentives + gamification |
| Proof of work | "Trust us" | Screen recordings of operators doing the work |
| Cost awareness | Surprise bills | Estimate before execution ("This will cost $3. You good?") |
